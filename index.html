My Solution

The Pocket Planter

A tiny, easy tool that works like a mix between a PEZ dispenser and a water squirter — letting you plant seeds anywhere you find soil.

  • 🌱
    PEZ-style seed dispenser

    Shake, lift, and drop seeds precisely where you want them.

  • 💧
    Built-in water squirter

  • 🪵
    Digging stick included

    Everything you need fits right in your pocket.

How It Works

Four steps to grow

Simple enough for a 5-year-old. Smart enough to actually work.

01

Fill the dispenser

Make sure the seed dispenser is loaded with your customised seeds.

02

Dig a hole

Use the included stick to dig a small hole in any bed of soil you see.

03

Dispense the seed

Give a nice shake, lift open the head of the PEZ, and drop your seed in.

04

Hydrate & grow

Squirt some fertilised water to hydrate your seed — then watch it grow!
