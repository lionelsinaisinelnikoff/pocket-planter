warning The Dilemma

While eating watermelon, I spat out the seeds and realized they could grow into something amazing. So I created the Pocket Planter — a tiny tool that helps you plant seeds anywhere.

lightbulb My Solution

To fix this problem, I created the Pocket Planter — a fun, easy tool that works like a mix between a PEZ dispenser and a water squirter, letting you plant seeds anywhere.

How It Works

1

Fill the Dispenser

Make sure that the seed dispenser is full of your favorite seeds.

2

Prepare the Soil

Dig a hole in any bed of soil you see with your stick.

3

The PEZ Shake

Give a nice shake and lift open the head of the PEZ to drop the seed.

4

Hydrate Your Seed

Squirt some water to hydrate your seed and start the magic!
