Chapter One

Rafael's Story

While eating watermelon, I spat out the seeds — and realised they could grow into something amazing.

format_quote

Most people would have wiped their hands and moved on. Rafael Sinai-Sinelnikoff looked closer. Those tiny black seeds weren't rubbish. They were the start of trees, shade, and life — if only planting were as simple as taking a walk.

"I spat out the seeds and realised they could grow into something amazing." — Rafael Sinai-Sinelnikoff, Founder

But tree planting rates are falling. Rafael saw it in the data — fewer hectares planted every decade. Why should growing green belong only to grown-ups with shovels and trucks? As a student at AAESS 7B (2025/26), building through Design & Technology, Rafael asked a bigger question: what if every kid aged 5 to 16 could carry a garden in their pocket?

That's how The Pocket Planter was born — a tiny tool that helps you plant seeds anywhere. Not someday. Not when you're older. Today — on the path to school, in the park, wherever you find a patch of soil.

Chapter Two

How It Works

Four simple moves. One living miracle. Rafael designed it to work like a PEZ dispenser meets a water squirter — fun enough for kids, clever enough to actually work.

1

Fill It Up

Make sure the seed dispenser is full — pop in your seeds and you're ready for adventure.

2

Dig Your Spot

Dig a hole in any bed of soil you see with your built-in stick. School path, park edge, garden bed — anywhere life can take root.

3

Shake & Drop

Give a nice shake and lift open the head of the PEZ — one perfect seed drops exactly where it should.

4

Squirt & Grow

Squirt some water to hydrate your seed. Walk away knowing you just planted something amazing.

Plant Anywhere

Turn every walk into a planting adventure — school paths, parks, gardens, anywhere soil meets sunlight.
